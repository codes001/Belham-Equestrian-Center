Who We Are

A DEDICATED TEAM OF EQUESTRIAN ENTHUSIASTS

At The Belham Equestrian Centre, our journey began with a profound love for horses and a desire to share the beauty of equestrianism with the community. Our founders, avid equestrians themselves, envisioned a place where people could connect with these majestic creatures, immerse themselves in nature, and experience the thrill of horseback riding. With unwavering dedication and a commitment to excellence, we transformed our vision into reality. The Belham Equestrian Centre was born, providing a sanctuary where riders can forge a bond with horses, learn new skills, and create lasting memories.

What is the minimum age requirement for horseback riding?

The minimum age requirement for horseback riding at our center is 11 years. However, please note that younger riders may have certain restrictions and need to be accompanied by a guardian.

Can I bring my own horse for training or boarding?

Currently, we do not offer training or boarding services for external horses. Our facilities and services are dedicated to the horses at The Belham Equestrian Centre.
